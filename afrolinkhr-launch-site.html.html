Questions people will ask

Use these answers to reduce friction before launch.

Is AfrolinkHR already live?

AfrolinkHR is in launch preparation mode. This site is designed to collect demand, explain the service, and support your next deployment steps.

Will employer details be public?

No. The platform is designed around controlled disclosure so sensitive identity and contact details can stay protected until screening has progressed.

How do candidates apply?

Candidates create profiles, review opportunities, and submit applications through the platform instead of relying on scattered external messaging.

Can the service include training or visa support?

Yes. The broader AfrolinkHR offer can include candidate readiness, employer vetting, and post-offer relocation or visa coordination support.
